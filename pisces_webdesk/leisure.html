Housewife

Leisure --> Short Stories


Mrs Sanvi: Congratulations Mrinal! Wish you a very happy married life, my sweetie pie.

Mrinal: Thank you very much, aunty.

Mrs Sanvi: I'd never expected that you'll marry so early. You just completed your B.Tech I guess!

Mrinal's mother: Oh come on! Mrs Sanvi, She is already eighteen plus. Girls at this age in my time used to have two kids.

Mrs Sanvi: She was throughout a meritorious student and this is the time to live her dreams.

Mrinal's mother: Yes, she has a brilliant mind. After all, she's my daughter. But you know we've found a groom for her who can fulfil all her wishes. She would live her dreams with him. She would live a luxurious life. Her to be in-laws are filthy rich. We are very happy and relaxed to give her in their hands.


-------------Marriage Ceremony took place.-------------

At in-laws house

Nitin(Mrinal's husband): Let's paint the town red.

Mrinal: Yes, it is a very special day for me. But I am too exhausted. It was yet a tiring day.

Nitin: I thought you might have planned some surprise for me. But you started babbling your own reasons.

Mrinal: I didn't mean to offend you.

Nitin: Oh! Stop giving excuses. It seems your mother has didn't taught you the qualities of a woman - the art of smile, the respect for others in her eyes, the tenderness and devotion.

Mrinal: I .......


A few days later...

Mrinal's mother(On a phone call): I hope you are trying to adjust. Always remember that they are now your family members.

Mrinal: Yes, mama everyone is very good here. They care for me a lot. I am working hard to gain a place in their hearts.


Few months later...

Nitin: Mrinal, I came to know that you uploaded your job profile that too without asking me.

Mrinal: I have very less work to do. Servants are hired for various works. So, I thought of utilising my spare time. It's just a part-time job.

Mrs Ghosh (Mrinal's mother-in-law): What is the need for working! You are daughter-in-law of our family. If people in the town would come to know that our daughter-in-law is seeking for jobs, we would be ashamed.

Mrinal: I want to live my passion.

Nitin: I want a lady who can be a good housewife. I earn so much that can feed my in-laws as well.

Mrs Ghosh: You are from top to bottom laden with jewellery. You have a wardrobe full of clothes. Every opulence is at your feet. What else do you need?

Mrinal weeps!


Tweet:



Mrs Sanvi(calls Mrinal on seeing her tweet): Mrinal, How are you doing?

Mrinal: I am good aunty.

Mrs Sanvi: Are you all right? I hope your in-laws are good to you!

Mrinal: Yes, aunty everyone is very good to me. They care for me a lot.

Mrs Sanvi: I have a gut feeling that something is wrong. My girl! tell me the truth.

Mrinal(starts weeping): It feels I've lost myself. I try hard to keep everyone happy but while trying hard to get their acceptance the image of my own self has faded. (Weeps continuously).

Mrs Sanvi: My child! I am sending you the tickets to Kolkata. Come to my place we will resolve the matter together.


Six months later...

Mrinal working at CVC Engineering Limited, Kolkata.
